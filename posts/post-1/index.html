Another blog

Posted on Jul 18, 2020

Hello,

Aah so here we are. I think I have finally mustered the courage to start a blog. I had a multitude of reasons to start a blog, and I have been planning to start blogging for the past few years, but I guess I always ended up hitting dead ends. Alas! Here we are.

While I have taken the first step and started this, the question at hand is to maintain consistency. I remember talking to a friend about the difficulties of starting something and maintaining consistency. While he argued that starting is easy and maintaining consistency is difficult, to me, starting something new is difficult. Remember the fear you get in the pit of your stomach before committing to something new? Feeling that maybe you are not ready yet or perhaps, in this case, fear of things like “nobody will read my blog.” That fear, I guess, just cripples you and makes taking the first step much more difficult.

I guess for the past few years, when I wanted to put my words in public, I have always ended up holding myself from not publishing anything, because I was always aiming for perfection. I was(and I still am) afraid that I’m not a good enough writer. I was afraid that my ideas are not original.

But I have to take a leap of faith and jump into this now. As @visakanv would say, “To get better at writing, write 100 blog posts.”

Don’t get me wrong being consistent is difficult too, but I feel it’s easier to create feedback-loops and be consistent.

What will I be writing about? I have some ideas in mind but for now, I want to focus on writing about ideas in Theoretical Computer Science. I might venture into paper reviews or random musings on art or something I learned, but I intend to soon start a notebook for that.
