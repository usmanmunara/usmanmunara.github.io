About

Aug 11, 2020

Hi! I am Usman and I am a Computing Mathematics major at the City University of Hong Kong.

While I tinker with different spaces like Tools for Thoughts, my current research interest is in topics in Theoretical Computer Science like Quantum Information Theory and Complexity Theory. The exponential difficulty of having fault-tolerant quantum computers and the thought of possesing these powerful machines in our hands, which can not only solve some of the most complex problems we have but also simulate the universe is just intriguing to me.

On the side I like to read, write, and take long walks.

Currently, I am working with Prof. Penghui Yao @ Nanjing University as a Research Assistant on a research project at the intersection of Quantum Information Theory and Quantum Communication Complexity.
